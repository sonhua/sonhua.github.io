Full CV




I am a Project Assistant Professor (特任助教) in The University of Tokyo.

At UTokyo, I work with Dr. Toshiya Hachisuka in physically based rendering. I am also interested in 3D scene understanding using deep learning techniques.

Before UTokyo, I spent two wonderful years in Singapore University of Technology and Design, working with Dr. Sai-Kit Yeung. I completed my PhD at School of Computing, National University of Singapore in May 2015, under the supervision of Dr. Kok-Lim Low. I obtained my Bachelor degree at Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology in 2008.
