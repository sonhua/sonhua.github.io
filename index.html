Full CV




I am a postdoctoral researcher in Singapore University of Technology and Design. My research interests include physically based rendering, 3D scene reconstruction, modeling, and understanding.

I completed my PhD at School of Computing, National University of Singapore in May 2015, under the supervision of Dr. Kok-Lim Low. Before coming to NUS, I obtained my Bachelor degree at Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology in 2008.
